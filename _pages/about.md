## <span id="about">About</span>
Welcome to my homepage!

I am a first-year master's student in the [School of Computer Science, Wuhan University](https://cs.whu.edu.cn/), advised by [Prof. Hua Zou](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=6JMV8NEAAAAJ&hl=zh-CN). I received my bachelor's degree from the [School of Computer Science, Wuhan University](https://cs.whu.edu.cn/) in 2025.

My research interests include medical image analysis, 3D vision, and embodied AI. I am broadly interested in machine learning and computer vision, especially in developing methods for visual perception, 3D understanding, and intelligent embodied systems.
